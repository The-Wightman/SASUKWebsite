SAS UK

SAS UK is the United kingdom branch of the International SAS paintball family, The team attends multiple events every year across the country. SAS UK focuses on co ordination and teamwork to represent ourselves both on and off the field. The team strives to achieve the best in all of its players with new recruits having to prove themselves on the field to be considered for a position on the team.If you think what it takes to bear the mantle consider getting in touch either via the above email or by clicking the button below to visit our facebook page.
